Uganda

The Uganda division of Think Tree is dedicated to environmental sustainability by planting local tree varieties, promoting climate action through initiatives like tree planting with local communities, including primary school children and childcare nurseries, and collaborating with the Trees That Feed Foundation to support reforestation efforts.

Learn More >

Kenya

The Kenya division of Think Tree is dedicated to empowering local communities through sustainable forestry projects. We work closely with farmers and community groups to plant indigenous trees, promote agroforestry, and enhance biodiversity. Our efforts focus on creating lasting environmental and economic benefits.

Join Now >

Tanzania

In Tanzania, Think Tree focuses on restoring degraded landscapes and improving livelihoods through community-based tree planting initiatives. We partner with local villages to establish tree nurseries, reforest key areas, and promote sustainable land management practices that benefit both people and wildlife.
