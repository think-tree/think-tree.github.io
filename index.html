Uganda

The Uganda division of Think Tree is dedicated to environmental sustainability by planting local tree varieties, promoting climate action through initiatives like tree planting with local communities, including primary school children and childcare nurseries.

Learn More >

Kenya

The Think Tree Kenya division focuses on hands-on environmental fieldwork—surveying land, identifying suitable and semi-arid areas for planting, and monitoring tree growth from seedlings to maturity. The team also raises environmental awareness by educating communities on pollution, conservation, and the importance of protecting our planet for future generations.

Learn More >

Tanzania

In Tanzania, Think Tree focuses on restoring degraded landscapes and improving livelihoods through community-based tree planting initiatives. We partner with local villages to establish tree nurseries, reforest key areas, and promote sustainable land management practices that benefit both people and wildlife.
