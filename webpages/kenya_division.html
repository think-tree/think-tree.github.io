Kenya Division

The Think Tree Kenya division focuses on hands-on environmental fieldwork—surveying land, identifying suitable and semi-arid areas for planting, and monitoring tree growth from seedlings to maturity. The team also raises environmental awareness by educating communities on pollution, conservation, and the importance of protecting our planet for future generations.

Although the team has not yet conducted cleanup activities due to financial limitations, they have actively identified polluted sites in urban areas and are seeking collaborations with local authorities for waste management solutions. The division continues to push for both ecological restoration and environmental education.
